Who Am I?

I am a Web Designer born and raised (and currently based in) Lynchburg, VA. My journey into tech started after a successful but unfulfilling career in finance/accounting. With a budding interest in designing and building websites, I began taking courses inline with my interests and working with clients on projects.

I pay attention to details, and make sure that the final product makes myself and my clients proud. I am passionate about creating amazing stuff. I'm serious about my work, but I don't take myself too seriously. I love music. I frequent museums. Good food is my love language.
